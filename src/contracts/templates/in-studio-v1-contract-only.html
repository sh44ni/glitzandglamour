Section 12
Late Arrival Policy

This section governs day-of service delays and operates independently of the cancellation policy in Section 09. Individual participant lateness — where at least one participant is present and services have commenced or are expected to commence — is addressed here. Whole-booking no-shows — where the Client or all participants fail to appear — are governed exclusively by Section 09 (Cancellation Policy).

  • If the Artist is delayed due to an emergency or other circumstances beyond their reasonable control, the Client will be notified as soon as possible and the Artist will make every reasonable effort to fulfill all agreed services.
  • Grace Period (1–15 minutes late): If the Client or any individual participant arrives up to 15 minutes after their scheduled start time, the Artist will make reasonable efforts to accommodate that participant's service within the original time window. Services may be modified, shortened, or omitted as necessary to stay on schedule, without any price reduction or refund.
  • Late Arrival (16–29 minutes late): If the Client or any individual participant arrives 16 to 29 minutes after their scheduled start time, the Artist may, at their sole discretion, proceed with a shortened or modified version of the service, or decline to perform the service entirely. The full agreed price for that participant's service remains owed regardless of whether the service is shortened, modified, or declined. No refund will be issued.
  • Cancellation Threshold (30+ minutes late): If any individual participant's lateness causes a delay of 30 minutes or more beyond that participant's scheduled start time, the Artist may cancel that participant's services entirely at their sole discretion. The full agreed price for that participant's service remains owed and no refund will be issued. This threshold is objective and measurable, not discretionary.
  • Where client-caused lateness results in the Artist remaining at the studio beyond the originally scheduled end time, overtime fees will apply in accordance with Section 13 (Overtime & Delay Fees). The Client acknowledges that late arrival does not extend the scheduled window at no cost — any overrun caused by the Client's tardiness is billable overtime.
  • If client-caused delay prevents completion of services within the scheduled service window, the Artist may refuse further service without refund while retaining all amounts owed under this Agreement.
